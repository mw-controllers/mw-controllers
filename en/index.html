Supported Charging Standards

CCS

Combined Charging System (CCS) is the European and North American standard for DC fast charging, supporting both AC and DC charging through a single connector.

Read more

Type2

Type2 (IEC 62196 Type 2) is the European standard AC charging connector, widely used across Europe for home and public AC charging stations.

Read more

GB/T

GB/T is the Chinese national standard for EV charging, widely used in China and compatible with the growing Chinese EV market.

Read more

CHAdeMO

CHAdeMO is a DC fast-charging standard developed in Japan, widely adopted in Asia and supporting high-power charging capabilities.
