Type2 (IEC 62196-2)

European AC charging connector for home and public infrastructure, supporting single and three-phase charging.

Talk to us about Type2

Charging Controller Description

Supply voltage: 12V ± 5% Power consumption: < 5W Operating temperature: -40 to +80°C Dimensions (L-W-H): 125-90-40(mm)

Controller Features:

  • 1 galvanically isolated RS485 interfaces
  • 2 galvanically isolated digital inputs
  • 4 relay (5A) for implement direct function, 2 relay (5A) for direct actuator

Operating Modes:

The controller is designed for use as part of an electric charging station. It supports the AC charging standard IEC 61851, GB/T 18487.

The controller can independently control the power modules and also act as a slave device. Control of the power modules is delegated to the central controller. This mode is required for stations with power balancing.

Communication Protocol:

The primary control protocol is Modbus RTU. The register map can be modified upon customer approval.
